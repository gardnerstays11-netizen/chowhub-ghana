Build a full-stack food and dining discovery platform for the Ghanaian 
market called [APP_NAME]. It is a platform where users can find every 
type of food and dining establishment across Ghana — restaurants, fine 
dining, breakfast spots, brunch spots, chop bars, bars, lounges, cafes, 
bakeries, food stalls, fast food joints, hotel dining, buffets, 
rooftop dining, pop-ups, and more.

Users can discover places near them, view full listings, make 
reservations, place orders, and contact businesses directly via 
phone, website, WhatsApp, or social media.

There is no payment processing on the platform for now. All contact 
and transactions happen directly between the user and the business.

Choose the best modern tech stack for this project. Pick technologies 
that are production ready, best suited for a discovery/listing platform 
with maps, filters, and SEO, scalable, easy to deploy, and good for 
both web and mobile. Document your tech stack choices in the README.

## VENDOR SIGNUP & DYNAMIC ONBOARDING

### Signup page (/vendor/signup)
Collect only:
- Business name
- Owner/manager full name
- Phone number (primary contact)
- Email address
- Password
- City (dropdown: Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Tamale)

After submission:
- Send OTP to phone number for verification
- On OTP verified: create vendor account, 
  status = pending, redirect to onboarding
- Show clear message: "Your account is created. 
  Complete your listing to go live."

### Onboarding flow (/vendor/onboarding)
Show a progress bar across all steps.
Save progress after every step so vendor can 
return and continue if they close the app.
Use encouraging, plain language throughout.
Show contextual tips on each step.

## STEP 1 — Business details

Fields shown to ALL categories:
- Business category (required) — selecting this 
  triggers dynamic form behaviour for all steps
- Short description (max 300 characters)
  Tip: "Tell customers what makes your place special"

Fields shown based on category selection:

CUISINE TYPE (multi-select) — show for:
  restaurant, fine_dining, breakfast_brunch,
  bar_lounge, rooftop_bar, fast_food, food_stall,
  buffet, hotel_dining, pop_up, food_court
  Hide for: cafe_bakery, dessert_drinks, chop_bar
  Pre-select for chop_bar: Ghanaian, West African
  Options: Ghanaian, West African, Continental, Chinese,
  Lebanese, Indian, Italian, American, Japanese,
  Mediterranean, Mexican, Thai, Turkish, Fusion

DINING STYLE — show for:
  restaurant, fine_dining, hotel_dining, buffet
  Hide for all others
  Options: Casual, Formal, Fine Dining, Buffet,
  Self Service, Takeaway, Dine-in Only, Delivery

MEAL PERIODS (multi-select) — show for all categories
  Pre-select based on category:
  - breakfast_brunch: Breakfast, Brunch (others hidden)
  - bar_lounge, rooftop_bar: Dinner, Late Night
  - cafe_bakery: Breakfast, Brunch, Lunch
  - fast_food: All Day, Lunch, Dinner
  - dessert_drinks: All Day, Late Night
  Options: Breakfast, Brunch, Lunch, Dinner, 
  All Day, Late Night

PRICE RANGE — show for all categories
  For buffet: show additional "price per person" 
  field in GHS
  Pre-select for:
  - chop_bar: Budget, Moderate
  - fast_food, food_stall: Budget
  Options with GHS amounts:
  Budget (GHS 0-50), Moderate (GHS 50-150),
  Upscale (GHS 150-300), Luxury (GHS 300+)

HOTEL NAME — show only for: hotel_dining

YEAR ESTABLISHED — show for all (optional)

Show category-specific messages:
- fine_dining: "Fine dining listings require a dress 
  code and reservation policy to be set before approval"
- food_stall: "No website or social media? No problem. 
  A phone number is all you need to get started"
- bar_lounge, rooftop_bar: Show 18+ venue toggle
- pop_up: "Pop-up listings are temporary. You can 
  update your event dates anytime from your dashboard"
- chop_bar: Show all price amounts prominently in GHS
